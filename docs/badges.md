# ByteList Badge Catalog

## Rating Scale
- Ratings are integer values from `1` to `10`.

## Exploration
- First Byte: first order.
- Local Explorer: 5 unique restaurants.
- Food Pathfinder: 10 unique restaurants.
- City Sampler: 15 unique restaurants.
- Flavor Cartographer: 25 unique restaurants.
- Bay Area Foodie: 50 unique restaurants.
- ByteList Legend: 100 total orders.

## Influence and Popularity
- Trend Starter: at least one order copied.
- Taste Influencer: 10 total copies across your orders.
- Menu Architect: your order becomes top-ranked at a restaurant.
- Flavor Authority: 25 total copies across your orders.

## Review Quality
- Food Critic: 5 detailed reviews.
- Flavor Analyst: 10 detailed reviews.
- Dish Philosopher: 25 detailed reviews.

## Category and Discovery
- Boba Connoisseur: 5 boba drinks logged.
- Boba Cartographer: 7 unique boba shops.
- Spice Seeker: 5 spicy dishes.
- Dessert Hunter: 5 dessert places.
- Ramen Master: 5 ramen dishes.
- Niche Foodie: 3 restaurants opened in the last 3 months.

## Special and Rare
- Menu Hacker: customized order copied by other users.
- Flavor Scientist: 5 customized orders.
- Hidden Gem Hunter: review a restaurant with fewer than 5 reviews.
- Perfect Plate: dish keeps a 9+ average with at least 3 reviews.
- Speed Reviewer: 3 restaurants reviewed in one day.
- ByteList Pioneer: first review at a newly added restaurant.
